# Legal And Data Use

OpenTrade Registry is public-records infrastructure, not legal advice.

Rules for contributors and users:

- Do not bypass CAPTCHAs, login walls, or technical access controls.
- Prefer official bulk downloads and APIs over fragile page automation.
- Respect posted terms and robots guidance where applicable.
- Generated datasets should not be published unless redistribution is clearly allowed.
- Keep source URL, fetched time, caveats, raw record, and fingerprint with exported data.
- Absence from one source is not proof of absence of a license or absence of bad history.
- Avoid public accusations.
- Minimize unnecessary personal data.
- Consider redaction options if sources expose individual home addresses.

Use careful language in downstream products and reports: "No matching record was found in this source as of the checked time."
